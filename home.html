Agrinhouse: Iran's Premier Exporter of Fresh Fruits and Greenhouse Produce

Agrinhouse stands at the forefront of Iran's agricultural export industry, delivering the finest quality fresh fruits and innovative greenhouse products to markets worldwide. From the lush orchards of Iran to your doorstep, we ensure every shipment embodies excellence, sustainability, and freshness.

About Agrinhouse

Based in the heart of Tehran at Vanak Square, Agrinhouse has built a reputation as a trusted partner for international buyers seeking premium Iranian produce. Our commitment to quality begins in the fields, where we cultivate and harvest under strict sustainable practices. With decades of experience in fruit export, we specialize in bridging Iranian growers with global demand, offering reliable supply chains for B2B partners.

In addition to our core fruit lines, our greenhouse operations produce year-round vegetables and specialty items, ensuring consistent availability regardless of season. We proudly support humanitarian causes, including aid for Gaza's children through partnerships with organizations like UNRWA.

Our Premium Products

Explore our diverse range of fresh fruits and greenhouse produce, each selected for superior taste, nutritional value, and export readiness. Our products are harvested at peak ripeness and packaged to maintain integrity during global transit.

Kiwi

Juicy, nutrient-rich kiwis from Iran's fertile regions, packed with vitamin C and antioxidants. Ideal for health-conscious markets.

Oranges

Apples
